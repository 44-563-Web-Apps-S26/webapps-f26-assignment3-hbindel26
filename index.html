cooking creamy spicy pasta

  1. add olive oil to an oven-safe skilet over medium high heat until just smoking. Add sausage and onions and cook until lightly browned, about 4 minutes. add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  2. add, broth, tomatoes, cream, pasta, salt and pepper and stir. bring to boil, cover skillet, and reduce heat to medium low. simmer until pasta is tender, about 15 minutes
  3. Remove skillet from ehat and stir in 1/2 cup of cheese. Top with remaining cheese and sprinkle with scallions. briol until cheese is melted, spotty brown and bubbly.
